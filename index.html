About Rapunzel

Rapunzel is a princess who has been locked up in a tower her entire life by an evil witch who abducted her at birth and pretended to be her mother.

Rapunzel's characteristics

  1. She has magical hair with healing properties
  2. On her birthday, the kingdom of Corona releases thousands of lanterns to remember their lost princess
  3. She loves to paint
